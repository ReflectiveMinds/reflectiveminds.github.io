About the Studio

Reflective Minds Studio is a small, developer-led studio focused on robust, modular gameplay systems for Unity. Our flagship product, RPG Combat Toolkit (TBCS), helps teams ship grid‑based, turn‑based combat faster — with clean abstractions for actors, stats, abilities, overlays, and both 2D/3D modes.

Values

  • 💡 Clear architecture & modularity
  • 🧪 Battle‑tested editor tools
  • 🎯 Practical, asset‑store ready

RPG Combat Toolkit (TBCS)

A complete, modular framework for grid‑based, turn‑based combat in Unity.

🔧 Modular Architecture

Actors, stats, health, abilities, overlays, and animation drivers — all decoupled via service providers.

🗺️ 2D & 3D Modes

Single setting switches between XY (2D) and XZ (3D). Visualizers and overlays adapt automatically.

🎯 Targeting & AOE

Pluggable evaluators for shapes, ranges, and filters, with polished zone previews and line arrows.

📝 Editor‑first

Wizards, clean inspectors, and sensible defaults to speed up setup and iteration.
